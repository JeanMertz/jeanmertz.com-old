Ethical Engineering is a software consultancy company focused on delivering working and maintainable software solutions for organisations and individuals in an ethical manner.

I hold strong principles on how I use my skills to help others. Ethical standards to me dictate that you treat your customers and employees well, run your business for the betterment of the world, not the enrichment of the few, and don’t participate in anti-competitive practices.

In my experience, there are some industries that go against these principles. If you want to hire me, know that if you operate in any of these industries, you will have to help me understand why you are different. These industries are military, industrial livestock production, targeted advertising, global-scale “social media”, and other privacy-eroding industries.
